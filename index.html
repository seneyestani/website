Welcome to my website!
I'm a postdoctoral scholar at the University of Southern California, Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, in Dr Jiachen Zhang's Air Pollution-Climate-Equity (ACE) research group. For my research, I use chemical transport models and remote-sensed observational datasets to investigate air quality and climate impacts of different emissions sources (anthropogenic or natural) at regional and global scales. You can find more details about my research on Research page of this website. Before joining USC, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of California, Riverside. Also, I attended the University of Georgia and the University of Tehran for my graduate studies. Besides research, I like to play tennis, go swimming, watch movies, and listen to music in my free time.
